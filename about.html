Have A Look at These Fun Ideas for Your Own Mehendi Function!

Out of all wedding celebrations, one of the most fun and intimate functions is, without a doubt, the Mehendi function. Apart from the traditional henna application, this pre-wedding ceremony is the one where you can freely experiment with themes, colours, and activities! From quirky photo booths, hanging florals and bright colours to an inventive food menu, there’s so much you can plan with your luxury wedding planner to add a spark to this auspicious event.
